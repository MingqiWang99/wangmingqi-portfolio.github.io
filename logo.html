LENOVO SUPPLY CHAIN PLANNING IT

LOGO DESIGN



This work is a series of logos designed for three intelligent supply chain systems during my internship in Lenovo's Global Supply Chain department, covering Supply Chain Collaboration, Supply Chain Intelligence and Planning (Planning Plus).
The inspiration of the logos are extracted from the representative letters from the three systems of SCC, SCI and Planning (Planning Plus): C (Collaboration), I (Intelligence), P (Planning).
