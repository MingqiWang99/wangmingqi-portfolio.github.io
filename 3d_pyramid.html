Connection Project-Designing with SolidWorks

PYRAMID


Created with SolidWorks and KeyShot, this pyramid consists of four different components that are connected with a snap fit. I researched different connection methods in real life and chose that of a normal pen cap. Theoretically, this functions as a toy which can be taken apart and reassembled in different ways.
