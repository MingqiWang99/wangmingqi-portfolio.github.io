Road Weather is designed for users who love road trips. It could be dangerous and unpredictable when travelling long distances. This site works as a plastform for people to share their experience and information to help others having a safe and comfortable trip. Unlike many other travel guide websites, Road Weather puts emphasis on educating users to travel safely and be fully prepared for all kinds of conditions. Let's start your journey here!
