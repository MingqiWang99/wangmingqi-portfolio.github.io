MEDICINE EASE



Medicine ease is a drug management app designed for individuals and families. Many families have back-up medicines at home, but it’s hard to remember the expiration date and the remaining amount. Seniors often need to take medicines regularly, but sometimes forget to take them or to purchase them before they run out. Medicine Ease can scan and store the information on the drug package to help the user better record the expiration date, usage, and approximate finish date. Users can add themselves as well as family members on the app, manage each person’s profile separately and set reminders. In the long term, users can also view their annual medication records.
